Farm to Home - Account Deletion

This page provides instructions to delete your account for the Farm to Home app, developed by Suresh Kumar CH.

Steps to Delete Your Account (In-App):

1. Open the Farm to Home app and sign in.
2. Click on the Profile item in the bottom navigation bar.
3. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile page and select "Delete Account".
4. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your account deletion.

If You Have Uninstalled the App:

If you no longer have the app installed but wish to delete your account or data, please email us with the following details:

  • Your Name
  • Your Registered Phone Number

Send your email to: suresh@farmtohome.live.
Once we receive your email, our team will verify your information and process your request promptly.

Data Deletion Policy:

When you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers. This includes:

  • Your profile information
  • Order history
  • Preferences you have set

Any data retained will comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
